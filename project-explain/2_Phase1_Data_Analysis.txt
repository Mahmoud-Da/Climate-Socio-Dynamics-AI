File: src/phase1_pipeline.py
Role: The Data Scientist

WHAT THIS CODE DOES:
It acts as a bridge between raw chaos and clean signals.

1. THE PROBLEM: FREQUENCY MISMATCH
   - Weather data comes Daily or Monthly.
   - GDP and Population data come Yearly.
   - You cannot feed this into an AI. It needs a row for every single month.
   - SOLUTION: "Resampling and Interpolation."
     We took the yearly GDP dots and drew a smooth curve between them to create monthly estimates.

2. THE "WHY": GRANGER CAUSALITY TEST
   - This is the most professional part of Phase 1.
   - A Correlation Matrix (Heatmap) only tells us things move together (Correlation).
   - Example: Ice cream sales and Shark attacks both go up in summer.
     Does eating ice cream cause shark attacks? No. They are just correlated.
   - GRANGER CAUSALITY checks if "Past values of X help predict Y."
   - We proved that Past CO2 statistically helps predict Future Temperature.
     This validates our decision to use CO2 as an input feature.

3. NORMALIZATION (Visualization)
   - We plotted the data on a scale of 0 to 1.
   - Why? Because GDP is 500 Trillion and Temp is 15 degrees.
     If you plot them on the same chart, the Temp line would look flat.
     Normalization lets us see the trends of both simultaneously.
