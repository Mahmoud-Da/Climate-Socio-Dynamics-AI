WHAT THIS CODE DOES:
It turns a static math model into a dynamic product for humans.

1. THE AUTOREGRESSIVE LOOP (The "Crystal Ball")
   - How do we predict 50 years (600 months) into the future if we don't have data?
   - We use the "Loop Method":
     1. AI looks at 2024 -> Predicts Jan 2025.
     2. We take that Prediction (Jan 2025) and feed it back into the AI as "History."
     3. AI looks at Jan 2025 -> Predicts Feb 2025.
     4. Repeat 600 times.
   - This allows the AI to "walk" into the future one step at a time.

2. SCENARIO INJECTION
   - The AI needs 4 inputs: [Temp, CO2, GDP, Urbanization].
   - The AI predicts Temp. But who predicts CO2 and GDP?
   - WE DO. That is the "Scenario."
   - If the user selects "Green Revolution," we force the CO2 numbers to go down in the loop.
   - If the user selects "Status Quo," we force them up.
   - This shows how sensitive the Climate (The AI) is to Human Policy (The Scenario).

3. STREAMLIT (The Interface)
   - Python is usually just text on a black screen.
   - Streamlit converts Python scripts into a Website.
   - It allows non-coders (stakeholders, politicians) to play with the parameters
     without touching the code. This is "Model Deployment."
